The Graduate Trainee Programme 2022

About the Programme

As part of our efforts to our corporate social responsibility, we believed that the best way would be to take advantage of the skills and knowledge we have as a team, hence the Graduate Trainee Program. The program aims to equip recent graduates with soft technical skills, experience and certifications required to begin a career in the IT industry.

With the help of the different experts in our team, we put together a 9-month program that enhances an individual with capacity to begin their careers as a functional or technical consultant in Microsoft, Oracle, Database and Cloud & Infrastructure. The program entails very intensive training sessions with self-study materials, with assessments done at different times. In addition, with full supervision & guidance from our trainers, the trainee gets to experience practical challenging client environments within our clientele arena.

At the end of the program, the trainees gain globally recognized certification throughout the program, and even more exciting, the successful trainees have a guaranteed chance of starting their careers as Kobby employees.

What the programme offers

1. Application Functional Consultants

The trainees are trained to be functional consultants focused on delivery and implementation of the Oracle & Microsoft applications, mainly the ERP solution. At the end of the training, they can translate a clients’ needs to technical terms enabling the technical team in their development phase, and vice versa.

2. Application Technical Consultants/Developers

They work closely with the Oracle & Microsoft Functional Consultants to understand the clients’ needs, thereafter, coding them to provide technical solutions in complex operation environments.

3. Infrastructure & Cloud Support Engineers

The graduate trainees interested in starting their career in this area, learn to implement and support cloud solutions, hardware and networks. They learn how to maintain reliable operations of the production test and development environments for different applications and infrastructure, ensuring compliance with policies and standards.

What the Programme aims for

The company purposes to provide a free of charge platform for passionate individuals to train and build their careers as a consultant, administrator or an Infrastructure & Cloud Engineer. The trainees also get to learn directly from experienced consultants in the industry, as well as being in an actual client environment.
